Rental Details

  • Two day minimum rental over 15 Miles* and Minimum Weekly Rentals over 40 Miles. Except SUP or Kayak. Some exclusions may apply. Please inquire.
  • SUP / Kayak Paddles are not designed to float. $60 will be charged for lost paddle.
  • $100 deposit required at the time of reservation. Or total for rental if under $100.
  • Deposit is non refundable if rental is canceled less than a month prior to beginning of rental.
  • Credit / Debit Card required to rent.
  • Remaining balance of the rental must be paid prior to the first day.
  • Renter is responsible for damages to pontoon or equipment.
  • Cancellation of rental less than thirty days before reserved date forfeits the deposit.
  • Renter must be 21 years of age or older and have a valid driver's license.
  • Payments accepted via Visa, MasterCard, Discover, or Cash.
  • Comes with full tank of gas and is expected to be returned as such.
  • Delivery and pickup times will be set when reservation is made.
  • Damages will be charged to the credit / debit card on file. SUP & Kayak Damage beyond use $500-750 will be charged to replace unit.
  • Late return fees:
    • 5-30 minutes late will incur a $30 charge.
    • Beyond 30 minutes late is $1 per minute thereafter.
  • Delivery to and pickup from a public access is included in the rental fee unless specified on the rental agreement "Over 20 Miles".
  • Life jackets and throwable device are provided; please indicate the number and sizes needed. Sizes available are:
    • Large
    • Medium
    • Small
    • Infant

Pontoon Rental is Available in Brainerd, Nisswa, Pequot Lakes, Gull Lake, Whitefish Chain, Crosslake, Walker, Park Rapids, Grand Rapids, and many more areas. Give us a call to see if pontoon rental is available in your area.
